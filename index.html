Podium rules

❌ Not permitted

  • Sunglasses
  • Headwear
  • Flags
  • Mobile phones
  • Cameras or electronic devices
  • Bottles
  • Sports equipment
  • Political statements or items
  • Accreditation passes

✅ Exception

Headwear worn for religious purposes is permitted.

🤝 If you have any item

Pass it to the Medal Ceremonies Team for the duration of the ceremony.

📍 If something is thrown to you

If you receive an item while on the Field of Play or podium, pass it to the Medal Ceremony Athlete Escorts to hold until after the ceremony.

Failure to follow the rules may result in disqualification by Commonwealth Sport.
